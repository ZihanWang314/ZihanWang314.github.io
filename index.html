Research Interest

My research interest lies in several topics regarding Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning, including (training and analyzing) pre-trained language models, alignment of Artificial Intelligence (e.g., Factuality of AI Generated Content and debiasing techniques), and interpretable natural language processing.
