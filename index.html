Research Interest

My research experience lies in Natural Language Processing and Information Retrieval. Most of my previous work aims to achieve cross application between IR systems and language models, e.g., retrieval augmented LMs and LM-based IR. I'm currently interested in several topics of NLP and IR, e.g., interactive language models, interpretable language model systems, and AI alignment (e.g., debiasing techniques and generation factuality) .
